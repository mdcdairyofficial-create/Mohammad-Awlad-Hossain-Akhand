# Refund Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

At our Legal Management Application, we strive to provide the best service to our users. Please read our refund policy carefully before making any payments.

## 1. General Policy
All subscription fees paid for our services are generally **non-refundable**. Once a subscription plan is activated, the fee is considered earned and is not eligible for a refund.

## 2. Exceptions
Refunds may be considered only under the following exceptional circumstances:
- **Technical Failure:** If a payment was successfully processed but the service was not activated due to a technical error on our part, and we are unable to resolve the issue within 48 hours.
- **Duplicate Payment:** If you are charged twice for the same subscription period due to a system error.

## 3. Refund Process
To request a refund under the exceptional circumstances mentioned above:
1. Contact our support team at [Your Support Email] within 7 days of the transaction.
2. Provide your transaction ID, mobile number, and a brief description of the issue.
3. Our team will investigate the claim and respond within 5-7 business days.

## 4. Approval
If a refund is approved, it will be processed through the original payment method (e.g., bKash, Nagad) within 10-15 business days.

## 5. Changes to Policy
We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our platform.
