# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our Legal Management Application ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when using our platform.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect information to provide better services to all our users.
- **Personal Information:** Name, mobile number, email address, and professional details (for Lawyers/Clerks).
- **Case Information:** Case numbers, court details, case status, and related documents uploaded by Lawyers/Clerks.
- **Usage Data:** Referral links clicked, points earned, and payment history.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To manage and provide access to case files.
- To track referral progress and reward points.
- To process subscription payments and recharges.
- To send important notifications regarding case updates.

## 3. Data Protection and Security
We take the security of your data seriously.
- All sensitive data is encrypted.
- We use industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access.
- Documents are stored in secure cloud storage, and we only store the access links in our database.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- **Payment Gateways:** To process your subscription/recharge payments.
- **Cloud Service Providers:** To securely store uploaded case documents.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Please contact our support team for assistance.

## 6. Compliance
We comply with the applicable data protection laws of Bangladesh.
